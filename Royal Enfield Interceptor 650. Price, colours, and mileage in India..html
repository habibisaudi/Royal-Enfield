        Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow.

        Along with the classically-styled variants that were inspired by its iconic namesake from the 1960s, this new version of the Royal Enfield Interceptor 650 moves the styling forward by a decade, into the 1970s. Built with a perfect balance of style and performance, its a modern classic that retains the essence and appearance of an authentic British roadster, but with a splash of sun-soaked California.

        /-

        The Blacked Out Interceptor 650

        The all-new, all-black Royal Enfield Interceptor 650 features a blacked-out engine and exhaust that unite the aesthetics of the motorcycle, seamlessly fusing with its chassis and keeping the spotlight on its tank for a look that is guaranteed to make hearts race and heads turn.

        /-

        At the Heart of it All

        The Royal Enfield Interceptor 650 is powered by Royal Enfield's legendary parallel twin cylinder engine, backed with an air/oil-cooled parallel twin configuration. The 648cc engine offers 47 horsepower at 7150 RPM and a maximum of 52 Nm of torque at 5250 RPM. A smooth throttle response throughout the rev range ensures sufficient power to make light work of the city traffic or cruise effortlessly along the open roads.

        /-

        Smooth Operator

        The Interceptor 650s wide handlebar, low seat height and confidence-inspiring riding position have been engineered for comfort with a gentle sports bias. Together, they help the rider make the most of the agile chassis and stay connected to the machine. With a 24-degree rake angle and 106 mm of trail, the motorcycle feels nimble in the city, planted on the highways and confident on the twisties.

        Interceptor 650 FAQs

        • What is the mileage of Royal Enfield Interceptor 650 Motorcycle?

          The Royal Enfield Interceptor 650 Motorcycle offers a mileage of approx 23.75 kmpl; however, it varies from weather conditions and the riding style of every ride.

        • What is the top speed of a Royal Enfield Interceptor 650?

          The top speed of the Royal Enfield Interceptor 650 is 164 km/hr.

        • What is the price of Interceptor 650 Motorcycle in India?

          The EX-Showroom price of the Interceptor 650 ranges from INR 3,03,000*.

        • What are the accessories for Royal Enfield Interceptor 650 Motorcycle?

          To check out the Genuine Motorcycle Accessories for Royal Enfield Interceptor 650 motorcycle, click here.

        • What is the technical specification of Royal Enfield Interceptor 650?

          The Interceptor 650 is powered by Royal Enfield's legendary parallel twin cylinder engine, backed with an air/oil-cooled parallel twin configuration. The 648cc engine offers 34.9 kW at 7250 RPM and a maximum of 52 Nm of torque at 5150 RPM. The Interceptor 650's wide handlebar, low seat height and confidence-inspiring riding position have been engineered for comfort with a gentle sports bias. Together, they help the rider make the most of the agile chassis and stay connected to the machine. With a 24-degree rake angle and 106 mm of the trail, the motorcycle feels nimble in the city, planted on the highways and confident on the twisties.

        • What is the kerb weight of Interceptor 650 Motorcycle?

          The kerb weight of the Interceptor 650 is 218 kg.

        • What are the colours options available for Royal Enfield Interceptor 650?

          The Interceptor 650 is available in 7 colours Black Ray, Barcelone Blue, Black Pearl, Sunset Strip, Canyon Red, Call Green, and Mark 2

        • What are dimensions of Interceptor 650 Motorcycle?

          With a length of 2,119 mm, a width of 835mm, and a height of 1,067mm, the Interceptor 650 weighs up to 218kgs. Moreover, the motorcycle is equipped with a wheelbase of 1398mm which leads to the motorcycle offering a ground clearance of 174mm.

        • Is Interceptor 650 BS6 Compliant?

          Yes, the BS6-compliant variants of the Interceptor 650 has been launched.

        • How many gears does Interceptor 650 have?

          The Interceptor 650 comes in with a 6 speed manual transmission.
